Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our WhatsApp Bot Service, We may collect the following personally identifiable information:

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number for communication purposes
  • Display Name: The name you provide during bot registration
  • Messages: The content of messages you send to our bot for processing commands
  • User Role: Your assigned role (USER or ADMIN) within our system
  • Registration Status: Whether you are actively registered with our service

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the WhatsApp Bot Service and may include:

  • Message timestamps and interaction logs
  • Command usage patterns and frequency
  • Service response times and performance metrics
  • Error logs for service improvement purposes

Use of Your Personal Data

The Company uses Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our WhatsApp Bot Service, including processing your commands and responding to messages
  • To manage Your Account: to manage Your registration as a user of the WhatsApp Bot Service
  • To contact You: To send automated responses via WhatsApp regarding your requests and service updates
  • To improve our Service: analyzing usage patterns to enhance bot functionality and user experience
  • For security purposes: to monitor for abuse and maintain system integrity

Data Storage and Security

Your Personal Data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: Our encrypted database provider for secure data storage
  • Railway: Our secure server infrastructure provider
  • Industry-standard security practices including encryption and access controls

Your information is processed in Canada and may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of Your province or country where data protection laws may differ.

Sharing of Your Personal Data

We may share Your personal information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: Supabase (database), Railway (hosting), and Meta/WhatsApp (messaging platform)
  • For legal compliance: if required by law or valid government requests
  • For business protection: to protect our rights, users' safety, or investigate potential violations

We do NOT sell or share your personal information for marketing purposes.

Retention of Your Personal Data

We retain Your Personal Data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Users: While you remain registered with our WhatsApp Bot Service
  • Unregistered Users: Your data is permanently deleted when you use the "unregister" command
  • Usage Data: Generally retained for shorter periods for service improvement and security purposes

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Use the "status" command to view your stored information
  • Delete Your Data: Use the "unregister" command to permanently remove your data
  • Stop Service: Stop using the bot at any time to cease data collection
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Our WhatsApp Bot Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us.
